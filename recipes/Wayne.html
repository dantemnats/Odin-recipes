Wayne's steak recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 inch thick Alberta beef Top Sirloin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Let sit for 10-15 minutes until room temperature
  2. and pepper heavily
  3. Grill at 400 4 minutes total
  4. Flip each minute to get grill marks
  5. Let sit for 2 minutes
  6. Down the hatch
